CIC Leadership Playbook

Courageous • Innovative • Collaborative Rituals for Everyday Leadership

Purpose

This playbook helps managers shape the daily employee experience—so work feels clear, meaningful, and energising.

Grounded in the principles of The One Minute Manager, it focuses on small, consistent actions that build trust, clarity, and performance.

Core Philosophy

Clarity creates confidence.

Recognition drives energy.

Feedback builds growth.

Daily Rituals

Courageous

1. One Minute Alignment

Start the day ensuring your team knows what success looks like.

  • What are the top 1–3 priorities today?
  • What does “done” look like?
  • What might block progress?
“Today, the most important thing is X. If we achieve Y outcome, we’re in a good place.”
Collaborative

2. Micro Check-ins

Short, informal touchpoints to stay connected.

  • Ask one meaningful question
  • Listen fully
  • Remove one blocker
“What’s one thing slowing you down right now?”
Innovative

3. Real-Time Recognition

Catch people doing things right—immediately.

  • Be specific
  • Tie to impact
  • Make it timely
“The way you handled that stakeholder was clear and calm—that helped move things forward.”

Weekly Rituals

4. One-on-One Conversations

  • What’s going well?
  • What’s frustrating?
  • What support do you need?

5. Constructive Feedback

Address issues early, clearly, and respectfully.

“When X happened, the impact was Y. Let’s align on how we approach this going forward.”

Monthly Rituals

6. Growth Conversations

  • Skills to build
  • Opportunities to stretch
  • Career direction

7. Team Reflection

  • What worked?
  • What didn’t?
  • What will we change?

Emotional Experience Map

Clarity → “I know what matters.”

Recognition → “I feel valued.”

Feedback → “I am growing.”

Connection → “I belong.”

Anti-Patterns

  • Only giving feedback when things go wrong
  • Unclear or shifting priorities
  • Delayed recognition
  • Avoiding difficult conversations

Quick Start Checklist

  • Set 3 clear priorities daily
  • Recognise 1 person per day
  • Have 1 meaningful check-in
  • Give feedback within 24 hours
